Las Vegas

Our first time in Vegas: truffle potato chips to die for at Emeril's Delmonico, Hannah shattered a $100 glass of vino, and Bryan actually danced!

I first met Hannah in the 6th grade over a discussion of Halloween costumes and we instantly became friends. Hannah became part of my family. It seemed we spent everyday together after school. She either came to my house or I went to hers. We both were fans of Indiana Jones. We went to school together all the way through high school. Our paths separated when we went to college but we never lost touch. Hannah will always be my friend. She is a person where we can go months without talking, but pick up a conversation as if no time has passed. When Hannah told me she had met Bryan I could not wait to meet him. I wanted to meet the man who made my friend so happy. I am yet to meet a couple that compliments each other the way that these two do. I am so happy to be part of their special day.

So with that said, here is an Irish quote for my Irish friend:

May God be with you and bless you. 
May you see your children's children. 
May you be poor in misfortune, rich in blessings.
May you know nothing but happiness. 
From this day forward.
-Sarah Taxdal

I remember the night you two first met (or maybe it was the first time I saw Bryan). It was quite the romantic scene: a squalid bug-infested couch in a college-town backyard, the two of you squished intimately in one corner.
